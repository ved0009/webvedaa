Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? You’re not alone. We’ve answered the most common things people ask—whether you’re curious, confused, or just want quick info. We help you launch your business online with website design, hosting, email, branding, and promotion—all done for you.

I don’t know anything about websites. Can I still get one made?

Yes — you don’t need to do anything. We handle everything from domain setup to design, hosting, email, and launch. Just tell us what your business is about.

What all will I get with my website?

You’ll get a fully responsive website, your own domain name, business email ID, business card (digital or printable), and even support with ads or marketing if needed.

How long does it take to build my website?

For most basic business websites, we deliver within 3 to 5 working days. For custom or advanced features, we’ll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Is there any hidden cost after delivery?

No hidden fees. We tell you the total cost upfront, which includes everything — domain, hosting, development, email setup. Optional extras are also quoted transparently.

Will I be able to update the website later?

Yes, we offer support even after delivery. You can either ask us to make changes, or we can give you a panel to update basic info like text, images, or services.

What if I don’t have a logo or content?

No problem. We can create a simple logo, write content for your website, and guide you through what’s needed to launch. You don’t need to come prepared with everything.
